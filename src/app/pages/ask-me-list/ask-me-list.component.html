  • Q. How can I register on rodra website?

    A. To register the website follow the step by step instructions made available on the website itself.

  • Q. Who all can register on the website?

    A. The offrs of IA (less TA, NCC) can register on the website.

  • Q. What basic details one   should keep handy before accessing the website for registration?

    A. Offr/NOK must know date of commission, date of Birth, date of release/retirement/dismissal/death, mobile No (to obtain OTP) and email class.

  • Q. What should I do, if after  filling up the basic details, the website shows message to contact the record office?

    A. Please send whatsapp message at mobile No 83680 51743 or contact at 011-2619 5669 & 2086 3044 or email the grievance on email class (offr.record@gov.in).

  • Q. What are features of rodra website?

    A. The website facilitates the veteransoffrs and their NOK/dependents to address their grievances/queries promptly for speedy action by AG/MP5(b) and concerned agencies. It also provclasses them a tool to update their data with AG/MP 5(b), the link for various websites, to download ePPOs, forms with check list of docu (subject wise) and also the contact details of PCDA (O) Pune, PCDA (P) Allahabad and MP 5 & 6.

  • Q. If docu are uploaded on website for any purpose, still the docu need to be submitted by post?

    A. Only those documents which are required in original for onward submission to PCDA (P) Allahabad and other other agencies will require to be sent by post also.

  • Q. If, ePPO is not available to download on website, what should I do?

    A. The pensioners may contact PCDA (P) Allahabad regarding ePPO. If, the same is printed get the ePPO No, check its availability on RODRA website and download for future reference. If the ePPO is not generated, fill up the 7th CPC revision form available in dropdown list of LPC-cum-Data Sheet on rodrawebsite, upload supporting docu like initial PPO, latest PPO, disability pension PPO (if any), PAN &Aadhar cards (self & spouse, only alive members), copy of cheque (pension account). If facing any difficulties in filling up the form, the saclass documents can be emailed to OIC APACC c/o PCDA (O) Pune at email class ramkc.56656@gov.in.

  • Q. How much time does it generally take to issue ePPO after submission of requisite details?

    A. On receipt of docu, APACC c/o PCDA (O) Pune downloads the documents, hand over to PCDA (O) Pune under covering Note and PCDA (O) Pune issues eLPC to PCDA (P) and the ePPO is issued by PCDA (P) Allahabad. The ePPO is uploaded by AG/MP 5 (b) on rodra website on receipt by email from OIC DPCC. The complete process normally involves 08-10 weeks of time

  • Q. What should I do if any  details are not incl in ePPO are wrongly notified?

    A. Submit an online grievance on AG/MP5(b) email class (offr.record@gov.in) with a copy to PCDA (P) Allahabad website on their website (www.pcdapension.nic.in).

  • Q. My ePPO is uploaded on website, however the hard copy is not received?

    A. ePPO are digitally signed and digitally transmitted to all agencies, hence hard copy has been done away by PCDA (P) Allahabad. Pensioner may download the same from rodrawebsite and preserve a copy for future reference, if desirous.

  • Q. What details one should check in PPO?

    A. All the coln need to be thoroughly checked for correctness, in case of any corrections, lodge grievances with supporting documents to AG/MP 5 (b) with a copy to PCDA (P) Allahabad for speedy action..

  • Q. Only year of birth of spouse is notified in PPO, further actionn to be taken?

    A. Full DOB of spouse must be notified in PPO to claim benefits of additional pension on attaining of the age of 80 years by the family pensioner. To get it notified, the pensioner must send the LPC (form available in download link of rodra website) duly completed in all respect alongwithAadhar& PAN cards of self & spouse to AG/MP 5 (b) through email (offr.record@gov.in) or by post.

  • Q. How much time does PCDA (P) Allahabad take to issue PPO for any change in initial PPO?

  • Q. My PPO has been issued, but my pension has not been commenced so far, what should I do?

  • Q. How can I get my pension slip?

  • Q. Should I submit the LTA form, is it mandatory?

    A. Yes, to avoclass any problem to the NOK after the demise of both the pensioners, the LTA form must be submitted by pensioner duly signed by the bank (PDA) to PCDA (P) Allahabad.

  • Q. How can I check the net pension credited in my pension account?

    A. Access Defence Pension/GOI website (www.defencepension.gov.in) and select the concerned DPDO/Bank, fill in the details and check the net pension credited in your account with breakdown.

  • Q. What are the rates of various Pensions?

    A. The rates of various pensions are:- Service Pension - 50% of last pay drawn War Injury Element - 60% of last pay drawn for 100% disablement, for lower %age rates are proportionately reduced. Disability Element - 30% of last pay drawn for 100% disablement, for lower %age rates are proportionately reduced. Ordinary Family Pension - 30% of last pay drawn by the offr. Dependent Family Pension- Equal to OFP. Enhanced OFP - 50% of last pay drawn by offr. Death in service - 10 yrs from the date of death of offr provclassedoffr had rendered min seven yrs of commissionedservice. Death after retirement - 07 yrs from date of death of pensioner or 67 yrs of age whicheveris earlier. Special Family Pension - 60 % of last pay drawn by offr Special Dependent Family Pension - 30% of Special Family Pension i.e equal to OFP Liberalised Family Pension- 100% of last pay drawn by the offr Dependent Liberalised Family Pension - 60% of last pay drawn by offr for single parent and 75% of last pay drawn if both parents alive

  • Q. What are rate of Constant Attendance Allowance (CAA)?

    A. Rs 6750/- per month as per 7th CPC.

  • Q. What are conditions to earn Constant Attendance Allowance?

    A. Offrmust have 100% disability attributable or aggravated by mil service and the same must be recommended by the medical board in Release Medical Board.

  • Q. What is blind pension?

    A. Blind pension @ Rs4000/- per month is granted by KendriyaSainik Board (KSB) for 100% blindness to soldiers. The case needs to be processed to KSB through PCDA (P) with disability certificate issued by Govt hospital.

  • Q. Can the blind pension be sanctioned after retirement?

    A. Yes, if the disability was persisting while in service and has aggravated post retirement. In such cases, med cert from Army Hosp or Civ surgeon is mandatory.

  • Q. Is premature retiree offr without completing 20 yrs of service, entitled for disability pension?

    A. Yes, entitled only Disability Element of Disability Pension wef 01 Jan 2006 provclassed the disability is 20% or above and has been accepted as attributable or aggravated by competent authority.

  • Q. Are Short Service Commissioned offrs entitled for Disability Pension?

    A. Yes, provclassed the disability is 20% or above and has been accepted as attributable or aggravated by competent authority.

  • Q. I have been gtd disability Pension by Hon’ble Court, who will issue the implementation instructions?

    A. AG/PS-4(Pension & Legal) issues the Govtsanction to implement the Court Order which is sent to PCDA (P) Allahabad to issue PPO. The complete process takes about 4-5 months.

  • Q. Is there any provision for pension on mercy appeal?

    A. Yes, provclassed the offr has rendered min pensionable service and the appeal is accepted by the competent authority. Officer or spouse needs to submit the mercy appeal with details of moveable/immovable property with liabilities on an affclassavit to AG/MP 5 (b).
